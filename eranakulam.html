ERANAKULAM

Ernakulam is a vibrant city located in the state of Kerala, India, and is often considered the commercial capital of the state. It is part of the Kochi metropolitan area, which has a rich history as a trading port and a melting pot of various cultures

Fort Kochi

Fort Kochi is one of the most famous tourist areas in Ernakulam, known for its colonial architecture, charming streets, and historic sites

Marine Drive

Marine Drive is a picturesque promenade along the backwaters in Ernakulam. It's an ideal place for a peaceful walk, enjoying views of the water, and witnessing beautiful sunsets. The area is also home to several shopping malls and eateries, making it a great spot to relax and explore.

Cherai Beach

Situated about 25 kilometers from Ernakulam, Cherai Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Kochi. The golden sand, gentle waves, and clean atmosphere make it ideal for swimming, beach walks, and water sports

Bolgatty Palace

Located on Bolgatty Island, Bolgatty Palace is a former Dutch colonial palace that has now been converted into a luxury hotel. The palace is set amidst beautiful gardens and offers stunning views of the backwaters.
